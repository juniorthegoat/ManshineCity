The Club

Built On Shine

Every club has a colour. We picked a wattage.

Manshine City just kicked off — a brand new club built on one idea: play like the floodlights are already on, from day one. No history yet. No trophies yet. Just a crest, a name, and a squad we're building right now.

We're aiming to be an attacking, high-tempo side built around movement and confidence on the ball. This page is the club's home base while we lock in players, a home ground, and our first fixtures. This is where the shine starts.
