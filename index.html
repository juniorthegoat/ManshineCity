The Club

Built On Shine

Every club has a colour. We picked a wattage.

Manshine City started in a car park with two goalposts made of jackets and one rule: play like the floodlights are already on. Seven seasons later, the jackets are gone, but the rule stayed.

We're a attacking, high-tempo side built around movement and confidence on the ball. Home games are loud. Away days are louder. This is where the shine comes from — showing up bright when it matters most.
